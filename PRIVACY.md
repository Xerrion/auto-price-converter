# Privacy Policy for Auto Price Converter

**Last updated: January 27, 2026**

## Overview

Auto Price Converter is a browser extension that converts prices on websites to your preferred currency. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

This extension:

- Does NOT collect personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing history
- Does NOT store or transmit website content
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools

## Data Storage

The extension stores only your preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

- Your selected target currency
- Display preferences (decimal places, show original price, highlight converted)
- Whether the extension is enabled

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Exchange Rates

The extension fetches current exchange rates from a public API. Only currency codes (e.g., "USD", "EUR") are sent in these requests. No personal or browsing data is transmitted.

## Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
